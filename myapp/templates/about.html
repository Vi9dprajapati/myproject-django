Project Aim

The primary aim of this educational platform is to create a comprehensive digital learning environment that bridges the gap between traditional classroom education and modern technological solutions.

Our platform provides a unified system for students, teachers, and administrators with AI-powered learning assistance, secure document management, interactive quizzes, and comprehensive progress tracking.

By leveraging cutting-edge technologies like Django, OpenAI API, and machine learning, we aim to enhance the educational experience for all stakeholders in the academic ecosystem.
